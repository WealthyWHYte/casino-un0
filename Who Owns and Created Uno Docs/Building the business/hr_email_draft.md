# HR Email Draft - CEO Visit Inquiry

## Subject Line Options:
1. **"Request for CEO Visit Schedule - Business Development Opportunity"**
2. **"Jim Allen Visit Schedule Inquiry - Strategic Initiative"**
3. **"CEO Meeting Schedule Request - UNO Gaming Innovation"**

## Email Draft:

**TO:** [HR Department Email]
**FROM:** [Your Name]
**SUBJECT:** Request for CEO Visit Schedule - Business Development Opportunity

Dear HR Team,

I hope this email finds you well. I am [Your Name], currently employed at Seminole Classic Casino in [Your Department/Position].

I am writing to inquire about the schedule for CEO Jim Allen's upcoming visit to our property on Tuesday, [Date]. I have been developing a significant business development opportunity that I believe would be of great interest to Mr. Allen and the Seminole Gaming leadership team.

**Background:**
Over the past several weeks, I have been researching and developing a comprehensive business proposal for introducing UNO Casino Gaming to our properties. This initiative represents a potential $13-20 million annual revenue opportunity for Seminole Gaming and could position us as the innovation leader in tribal gaming.

**Request:**
I would greatly appreciate information about:
- Mr. Allen's arrival time and general schedule for Tuesday
- Appropriate protocol for requesting a brief meeting (10-15 minutes)
- Best approach for presenting a business development opportunity
- Any advance materials or documentation requirements

**Professional Preparation:**
I have prepared comprehensive documentation including market analysis, financial projections, implementation strategies, and competitive research. This is a fully developed business case, not just an idea.

**Flexibility:**
I understand Mr. Allen's schedule is extremely busy. I am flexible and willing to work around any timing constraints. If Tuesday is not feasible, I would welcome guidance on the appropriate process for scheduling a future meeting.

**Confidentiality:**
This inquiry and the associated business opportunity are being handled with complete confidentiality and professionalism.

I would be happy to provide additional details about the nature of this opportunity if that would be helpful for scheduling purposes. Thank you for your time and assistance in facilitating this potential meeting.

Please let me know the best way to proceed.

Best regards,

[Your Name]
[Your Employee ID]
[Your Department]
[Your Phone Number]
[Your Email Address]
