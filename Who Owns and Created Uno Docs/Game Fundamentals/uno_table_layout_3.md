## Layout Specifications

### **Table Dimensions:**
- **Length:** 14 feet (stadium configuration)
- **Width:** 8 feet (tiered seating area)
- **Height:** Variable (30" base, 36" elevated dealer)
- **Shape:** Semi-circular stadium design

### **Player Arrangement:**
- **Total Capacity:** 12 players maximum
- **Tier 1:** 6 players (front row, closer to action)
- **Tier 2:** 6 players (elevated second row)
- **Sight Lines:** Unobstructed view for all players

### **Dealer Position:**
- **Elevation:** Raised 6 inches above player level
- **360° Access:** Central position with full table oversight
- **Equipment Station:** Integrated technology hub
- **Communication:** Wireless headset for floor coordination

### **Betting Areas:**

#### **Individual Player Bets:**
- **Main Game:** Personal betting circles for each player
- **Position Betting:** Bet on finishing position (1st, 2nd, 3rd)
- **Personal Bonuses:** Individual achievement bets

#### **Community Betting Area:**
- **Wild Card Pool:** Shared betting on wild card frequency
- **Draw Four Pool:** Community bet on +4 wild cards
- **Color Dominance:** Bet on most frequent color
- **Skip Chain:** Bet on consecutive skip cards
- **Reverse Rally:** Bet on direction changes
- **Draw Two Streak:** Bet on penalty card sequences
