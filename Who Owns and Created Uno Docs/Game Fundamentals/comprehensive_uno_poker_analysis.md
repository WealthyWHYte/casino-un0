### **Original UNO (1971)**
**Cards:** 108 total
- Number cards: 0-9 in four colors (Red, Yellow, Blue, Green)
- Action cards: Skip, Reverse, Draw 2
- Wild cards: Wild, Wild Draw 4

**Basic Rules:**
- Match color, number, or symbol
- First to discard all cards wins
- Must say "UNO" when down to one card
- Points scored for cards remaining in opponents' hands

## CORE POKER MECHANICS

### **Fundamental Betting Structures:**
1. **Fixed Limit:** Set bet amounts per round
2. **Pot Limit:** Maximum bet equals current pot size
3. **No Limit:** Players can bet any amount
4. **Spread Limit:** Betting within specified range

### **Betting Rounds:**
- **Pre-flop/Initial:** Before community cards
- **Flop/Second:** After first community cards
- **Turn/Third:** After additional card
- **River/Final:** After all cards dealt
