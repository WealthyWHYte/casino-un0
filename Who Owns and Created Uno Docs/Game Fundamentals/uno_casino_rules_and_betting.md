## BASIC GAME RULES

### **Objective**
Players compete to be first to play all cards while making strategic bets on outcomes.

### **Setup**
- **Players:** 3-6 players per table
- **Dealer:** Professional casino dealer manages game
- **Cards:** Standard UNO deck (108 cards)
- **Starting Hand:** 7 cards per player
- **Ante Bet:** Required bet to participate ($5-100 minimum)

### **Game Flow**
1. **Ante Phase:** All players place ante bets
2. **Deal Phase:** 7 cards dealt to each player
3. **Betting Round 1:** Initial betting based on hand strength
4. **Play Phase:** Standard UNO gameplay with betting opportunities
5. **Action Cards:** Special betting when Wild/Draw cards played
6. **Elimination:** Players eliminated when they can't play
7. **Winner Determination:** Last player or first to empty hand
8. **Payout Phase:** Winnings distributed based on betting pools

## BETTING SYSTEM

### **Primary Betting Pool (Like Poker)**

#### **Ante Bet (Required)**
- **Minimum:** $5-25 depending on table limit
- **Purpose:** Entry fee to participate in hand
- **Pool:** All ante bets create main prize pool
- **Payout:** Winner takes 70%, runner-up takes 20%, house takes 10%

#### **Progressive Betting Rounds**
Players can increase bets during play:

**Round 1 - After Deal:** Bet on hand strength
- **Strong Hand Bet:** 2x ante if holding multiple action cards
- **Color Dominance Bet:** 1.5x ante if holding 4+ cards of same color
- **Wild Card Bet:** 3x ante if holding Wild or Draw 4 cards

**Round 2 - Mid Game:** Bet on position
- **Leader Bet:** 2x ante if currently winning (fewest cards)
- **Comeback Bet:** 3x ante if behind but confident
- **Elimination Bet:** 1.5x ante on which player eliminated next

**Round 3 - Final Cards:** Last chance betting
- **Victory Bet:** 5x ante if down to 2 cards or less
- **Block Bet:** 2x ante to prevent specific player from winning
- **Desperation Bet:** 10x ante if down to final card
