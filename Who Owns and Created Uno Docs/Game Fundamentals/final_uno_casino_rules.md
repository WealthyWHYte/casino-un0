## DETAILED RULES

### **ANTE REQUIREMENTS**
- **Minimum Ante:** $5-25 (varies by table limit)
- **Purpose:** Creates initial pot for each hand
- **All Players:** Must ante to receive cards
- **Pot Distribution:** Winner takes 70%, runner-up 20%, house 10%

### **CARD DEALING**
- **Starting Hand:** 7 cards per player (face down)
- **Discard Pile:** 1 card face up to start play
- **Draw Pile:** Remaining cards face down
- **Dealer Button:** Rotates clockwise each hand

### **BETTING ROUND 1: HAND ASSESSMENT**
*After cards are dealt, before UNO play begins*

**Betting Options:**
- **Fold:** Forfeit ante, exit hand
- **Call:** Match current bet to continue
- **Raise:** Increase bet (2x ante maximum)

**Hand Strength Indicators:**
- **Strong Hand:** 3+ action cards, multiple colors
- **Medium Hand:** 2 action cards, good color distribution  
- **Weak Hand:** Few playable cards, single color dominance

### **UNO PLAY PHASE**
*Standard UNO rules with integrated betting opportunities*

#### **Turn Sequence:**
1. **Play a Card:** Match color, number, or symbol
2. **Betting Decision:** Option to place additional bets
3. **Draw if Unable:** Take cards from draw pile
4. **Say "UNO":** When down to one card

#### **Action Cards:**
- **Skip:** Next player loses turn
- **Reverse:** Change direction of play
- **Draw 2:** Next player draws 2 cards
- **Wild:** Choose new color
- **Wild Draw 4:** Choose color, next player draws 4

#### **Integrated Betting Opportunities:**
**When Playing Action Cards:**
- **Action Bet:** Bet that your action card will eliminate a player
- **Color Control Bet:** Bet on maintaining color control with Wild cards
- **Chain Reaction Bet:** Bet on consecutive action cards

**Position-Based Betting:**
- **Leader Bet:** Bet when you have fewest cards
- **Comeback Bet:** Bet when behind but confident
- **Elimination Bet:** Bet on which player eliminated next

### **BETTING ROUND 2: MID-GAME**
*Triggered when any player reaches 3 cards or fewer*

**Betting Options:**
- **Victory Bet:** Bet you'll win this hand (3x ante maximum)
- **Block Bet:** Bet against specific player winning (2x ante)
- **Wild Card Bet:** Bet on Wild cards determining winner (5x ante)

### **FINAL BETTING ROUND**
*When any player says "UNO" (down to 1 card)*

**Betting Options:**
- **Champion Bet:** Bet the UNO player will win (2x ante)
- **Upset Bet:** Bet someone else will win (4x ante)
- **Draw Out Bet:** Bet UNO player will be forced to draw (3x ante)

### **WINNING CONDITIONS**
1. **First to Empty Hand:** Primary winner
2. **Lowest Card Count:** If draw pile exhausted
3. **Last Player Standing:** If others fold from betting
